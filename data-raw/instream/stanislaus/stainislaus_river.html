Description of modeling TODO

Mark Gard instructed us to use I4:N60 of the Stanislaus tab within his IFIMWUA.xlsx These cells represent the sums of the rearing habitat in the canyon segment, knights ferry to orange blossom, orange blossom to jacomb myers, and jacob myers to san joaquin and then converted from sqft to sqft/1000ft. Spawning is modeled as one 50 mi segment.

Rearing WUA Plot

stanislaus_raw %>%
  filter(is.na(FR_spawn_wua)) %>%
  select(-FR_spawn_wua) %>%
  gather(species, wua, -flow_cfs) %>%
  ggplot(aes(flow_cfs, wua, color = species)) +
  geom_line() +
  labs(x = 'flow (cfs)', y = 'rearing WUA (sqft/1000ft)') +
  scale_y_continuous(labels = comma)
